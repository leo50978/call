About Golden Customer Services

Golden Customer Services is an interpretation and training agency focused on the Haitian Creole language. We were founded with the vision of providing high-quality interpretation services in medical, legal, and conference fields. Our team consists of native interpreters trained in professional and ethical standards.

Why Choose Us?

  • We speak your native language fluently
  • Our interpreters are certified and well-trained
  • Available 24/7 to meet your needs
  • Experienced with strong professional ethics
  • Proven training programs with real success

Our Mission

To facilitate access to clear and respectful communication through accurate, ethical, and compassionate Haitian Creole interpretation services, especially in medical, legal, and conference settings. We are committed to training professionals who serve our communities with experience, respect, and excellence.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in Haitian Creole interpretation and interpreter training, while continuing to build strong bridges between Haitian communities and the essential services they need. We believe in a world where everyone deserves to be understood — and to understand.

Our Services

Golden Customer Services is a professional interpretation and training agency dedicated to providing reliable and high-quality services tailored to Haitian Creole speakers' needs.

🩺

Medical Interpretation

We provide accurate, confidential interpretation for hospital visits, therapy, emergency care, and medical follow-ups. We bridge the communication gap between patients and providers.

⚖️

Legal Interpretation

We offer interpretation for court hearings, immigration, law offices, and legal depositions. Our services are discreet, ethical, and comprehensive.

🎤

Conference Interpretation

We interpret live or virtual events such as workshops, seminars, and international conferences — ensuring every participant gets the message clearly.

🏫

Interpreter Training Program

We train new interpreters to succeed in the local and global market with a complete curriculum:

  • • Medical interpretation
  • • Legal interpretation
  • • Over-the-phone and video interpretation
  • • Ethics and interpreting techniques
  • • Final exam and certification

Training is available online (remote) or in groups, with certificates awarded upon completion.

📞

Remote Interpretation Services

We offer phone (OPI) and video (VRI) interpretation services available 24/7. You can access our services from anywhere with just a phone or internet connection.

📝

Document Translation

We translate legal, medical, educational, and administrative documents with accuracy and confidentiality. (Available as an optional service upon request).
