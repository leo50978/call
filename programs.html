How Our Programs Work

Our training programs are designed to provide you with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed as a professional interpreter.

1

Application & Assessment

Submit your application and complete a language assessment to determine your current proficiency level.

2

Theoretical Training

Learn the fundamentals of interpretation, ethics, terminology, and techniques through our comprehensive curriculum.

3

Practical Exercises

Apply your knowledge in realistic scenarios with guided practice sessions and feedback from experienced instructors.

4

Certification Exam

Demonstrate your skills through a comprehensive final examination to earn your professional interpreter certification.

5

Career Placement

Receive assistance with job placement and career development opportunities upon successful completion of the program.
